Practical support for better organisations

For over 17 years, I've been helping organisations unlock their potential by creating stronger cultures, developing effective leaders, and building more engaged, high-performing teams. As the founder of RB Business Insights, I work with business leaders, senior management teams, and organisations of all sizes to identify barriers to growth and create practical strategies that drive lasting improvement. My experience spans more than 150 organisations across a diverse range of sectors, giving me a unique perspective on what makes businesses succeed. My expertise combines organisational development, employee engagement, leadership effectiveness, people strategy, customer experience, and business growth. I am also a registered Investors in People Specialist, supporting organisations through accreditation, assessment, and continuous improvement journeys. This blend of strategic consultancy and hands-on operational leadership enables me to bring both practical insight and commercial understanding to every client engagement. I believe that business success comes when strategy, people, and customer experience are aligned. My role is to help organisations make those connections, creating workplaces where people thrive and businesses perform at their best.
